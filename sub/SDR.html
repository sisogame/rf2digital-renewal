Steps of How to Implement HDRadio

  • Step 04

    Pay the royalty and NRE to Integrator

    The integrator is eligible to get paid for the framework and other S/W IP royalty as well as NRE for the integration work

  • Step 03

    Pay the royalty directly to Xperi

    When mass production starts, Tier1 has to pay the HDRadio SDR solution royalty to DTS directly

    License fees that have been paid to Xperi previously, excluding the SDR royalty, must continue to be paid as usual.

    Only the chipset price shall be replaced with the above solution royalty

  • Step 02

    Obtain the library and pass it on

    Xperi shall provide HDRadio library to the Tier1

    Tier1 has to provide the library to the integrator

  • Step 01

    Contract with Xperi to get HDR library

    A Tier1 customer wanting to implement SDR-based HDRadio must first establish a license contract directly with Xperi.
